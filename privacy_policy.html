Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Medixbot, a comprehensive telemedicine application designed to provide seamless healthcare services, including remote consultations, AI-powered health recommendations, continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) data integration, and secure patient-doctor interactions. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when using the Medixbot mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name, surname, date of birth, and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Government-issued identification (if required for verification)
  • Medical history, symptoms, and consultation records
  • Insurance and billing details (if applicable)

2.2 Health Data

  • Real-time glucose monitoring data (if connected to a CGM device)
  • AI-generated health insights and recommendations
  • Medical prescriptions, lab reports, and doctor’s notes
  • Patient-entered health metrics such as blood pressure, heart rate, and dietary habits

2.3 Usage Data

  • Device information (model, operating system, app version)
  • Log files, IP addresses, and geolocation (only with consent)
  • User interactions within the app (features accessed, time spent on the app)

2.4 AI and Telemedicine Data

  • Chat and voice interactions with AI medical assistants
  • Video and text consultations with healthcare providers
  • Data collected from AI-powered recommendation systems

3. How We Use Your Information

We collect and process your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide secure and efficient telemedicine services
  • To connect you with licensed healthcare professionals
  • To analyze health metrics and provide AI-powered recommendations
  • To enhance the functionality and security of Medixbot
  • To comply with medical regulations and legal requirements

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following cases:

  • With healthcare professionals for telemedicine consultations
  • With authorized medical institutions for laboratory tests or prescriptions
  • With AI analytics providers to improve health recommendations (data anonymized)
  • With regulatory authorities when required by law

5. Security of Your Data

We implement stringent security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for telemedicine consultations
  • Secure cloud storage compliant with HIPAA, GDPR, and other health data regulations
  • Role-based access control for sensitive medical information

6. Your Rights and Choices

As a user of Medixbot, you have the following rights:

  • Access & Correction: You may access and update your personal information
  • Data Deletion: You can request deletion of your data when legally permitted
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of non-essential data processing such as analytics

7. Retention of Data

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide healthcare services and comply with legal requirements. When no longer required, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Children’s Privacy

Medixbot is designed for users aged 18 and above. If a minor requires medical assistance, parental consent is required before data collection and telemedicine services.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated via in-app notifications or email.
